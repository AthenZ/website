Flava IAM: Bridging Security and Usability with Athenz

Overview

LY Corporation, formed by the merger of Yahoo! JAPAN and LINE, has adopted Athenz as the foundation for their access control infrastructure. As part of the merger integration, LY Corporation built Flava, a new internal private cloud platform, to consolidate and enhance the cloud infrastructure from both companies. This unification aimed to combine the best practices and strengths of each organization's cloud solutions while providing a cohesive platform for their services.

At Tech-Verse 2025, LY Corporation presented how they designed and built Flava IAM, the Identity and Access Management system for this new platform. The presentation showcased how Athenz serves as the core authorization engine, enabling Flava to achieve both strong security and practical usability in their real-world cloud environment.

About the Presentation

The session explained how Flava IAM leverages Athenz to achieve user-friendly experience and operational efficiency in real-world environments. The presentation covered their approach to combining the strengths of previous cloud IAM solutions from Yahoo! JAPAN and LINE, while highlighting how their design differs from traditional internal IAM platforms.

Key Features

  • IAM as a Balanced Broker: Balancing security and usability
  • Enhanced User Experience: Clearer views by user and role, flexible ways to manage roles, and built-in permission reviews
  • Provider Experience: Simplified provider integration through Athenz templates and provider catalog
